Our Story

MASTERPIECES LAVENDER TRAD. is a Sohar based company was established by professional in Mechanic Engineers having vast experience in the field of all kind of machines. Our every employees believes value of engineering is an important part of our work and we provide solution, not just products, our team qualified experienced engineers and technicians can create and build to client specifications whatever the challenges.
